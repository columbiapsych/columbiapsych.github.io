Welcome, Prospective Students!

We’re thrilled that you’re here! On this site, you will find everything you need to help you navigate through your (virtual) Prospective Student Visit with the Columbia University Psychology Department.

If any questions arise during your visit, please feel free to reach out to us any time, either by using our Slack channel, or by contacting one of the Prospectives Event coordinators via email: Jacob Edwards, Manasi Jayakumar, and Anna Vannucci.
