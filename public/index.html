Welcome, Prospective Students!

We’re thrilled that you’re here! On this site, you will find everything you need to help you navigate through your (virtual) Prospective Visit Days with the Columbia University Psychology Department.

If any questions arise, please feel free to reach out to us anytime, either by using our Slack channel, or by contacting one of the Prospective Visit Days coordinators via email: Jo He, Jiawen Huang, and Eivinas ButKus.

Welcome Video

Although we can’t welcome you to campus in person this year, we’ve put together a video to help introduce you to the Columbia campus, the psychology department, and the people within it.

How We’ll Connect

  • Slack: Ongoing support
  • Kumospace: Social events (links vary by event; see Schedule page)
  • Zoom: Individual and group meetings (links vary by meeting; see Schedule page for group meetings and your email for individual meetings)

For more information on these tools, see the Resources page.
