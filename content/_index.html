Welcome, Prospective Students!

We’re thrilled that you’re here! On this site, you will find everything you need to help you navigate through your (virtual) Prospective Visit Days with the Columbia University Psychology Department.

If any questions arise, please feel free to reach out to us anytime, either by using our Slack channel, or by contacting one of the Prospective Visit Days coordinators via email: Jo He, Jiawen Huang, and Eivinas ButKus.
